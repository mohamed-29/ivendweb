About Ivend

Ivend, a 100% Egyptian brand and proud subsidiary of Mobica, leverages over 45 years of trusted manufacturing expertise to deliver high-quality, reliable vending machines. Our extensive product range includes beverage units, snack dispensers, coffee machines, and reverse vending machines—each fully customizable in design, branding, and functionality.

Beyond these, we offer tailored vending solutions for a variety of sectors, including fashion, electronics, personal care, and more, ensuring that businesses can provide seamless, on-the-go experiences while maintaining a strong brand presence in any environment.
